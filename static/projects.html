HitMeUp.

Find out who's free at the click of a button.

With schedules changing every quarter, it becomes really difficult to find a mutual time slot to hang out with friends. That’s the motivation behind HitMeUp: a social network to solve the classic scheduling problem. As a user of this application, you’ll have a friends list with customizable privacy settings. You’ll be able to see when you can hit up other friends, whether it’s now or this Friday. The primary goals of HitMeUp are to allow students to easily hang out with any friends who are free at the moment and to easily find times when other people are available.
